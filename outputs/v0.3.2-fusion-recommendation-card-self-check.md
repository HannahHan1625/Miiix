## Product Judgment

The Warehouse flow now separates input and output responsibilities:

- Bottom tray: choose ingredients, tools, preferences, and regenerate.
- Recommendation card: inspect dish details, favorite, make, and add to today's cooking record.

This avoids turning the fusion output into another settings strip and gives the recommendation moment more emotional payoff.
